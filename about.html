About Layos
Beauty Hub

Layos Beauty Hub is built on a quiet confidence the kind that understands beauty is personal, intentional, and powerful. Founded with a belief in refined craftsmanship, our collections blend ethically sourced ingredients, careful formulation, and an aesthetic that feels both modern and timeless. We design for women who seek understated luxury: pieces and products that enhance, not overpower.

From our first small batch of lip balms created in a home kitchen, to a full line of perfumes and jewelry collections, Layo's journey is the result of curiosity, patient practice, and a commitment to quality. We prioritize skinfriendly formulations, elegant finishing, and packaging that transforms ordinary routines into moments of selfcare.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to empower women to feel confident and elegant through beautifully made products,perfumes that linger, cosmetics that flatter, and jewelry that complements. We envision a community where beauty is inclusive, sustainable, and thoughtfully crafted. Each product is an invitation to ritual and confidence.

Quality

We test and select ingredients that meet safety and performance standards so our customers can trust every product.

Sustainability

We pursue recyclable packaging, refill plans, and fair sourcing where possible to reduce environmental impact.

Community

Supporting local artisans and training small teams is part of our commitment to growing with the community.

The Craft Behind Our Products

Perfumes & Fragrances

Each scent is developed to have depth and longevity. Our perfumers blend top, heart and base notes to create perfumes that evolve with wear. We source sustainably and prioritize natural extracts where they enhance the composition.

Cosmetics & Makeup

Pigments are calibrated for rich payoff and skinfriendly formulas. Foundations and concealers are matched to diverse skin tones; lip colors and palettes are curated to feel luxurious and wearable.

Jewelry & Finishing

Our jewelry pieces are designed to be complementary and longlasting. Metals and stones are selected for durability and elegant finish. Each piece undergoes hand inspection before it reaches the collection shelf.

Packaging & Experience

Presentation matters: boxes, labels and unboxing are crafted to make customers feel celebrated. We aim for a minimalist, refined aesthetic.

Our Journey

  1. Humble Beginnings: Small batch lip balms handcrafted and gifted to friends and family. Early feedback shaped our first formulas.
  2. Local Launch: Popup markets and small boutiques helped Layo understand customer preferences and grow with intention.
  3. Refinement: Collaboration with cosmetic formulators and packaging designers helped professionalize the line.
  4. Expansion: Jewelry and perfume collections launched, along with small workshops for customers to learn product care.
  5. Community: A customer first approach that listens and adapts we continue to evolve with the women who wear our brand.
